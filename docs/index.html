About These Documents

The governing documents of Amberson Towers comprise three separate documents:

  • The Rules and Regulations - General rules which can be amended by a majority vote of the Council. Last amended in approximately September 2018.

  • The Declaration - Mostly detailed information about the building, such as surveyorâ€™s coordinates and voting share of each unit. Takes precedence over other documents. Requires agreement of 60% ow owners to amend. First filed on March 14, 1978 and amended twice, most recently September 12, 1978, but only with very minor changes.

  • The Code of Regulations - Regulates how the building is governed, for example how Council is elected. Requires agreement of 60% ow owners to amend. First filed April 12, 1978 and has been amended seven times (September 1978, 1990, 1994, 1996, 2000, 2016, and 2017).

Notes & Disclaimer: This publication has been produced for the convenience of our residents and users. It is an unofficial reproduction of the original documents and any ameendments thereto. This version of the Code of Regulations integrates all revisions into one consolidated document. The official recorded documents should be consulted for all interpretations and applications. They are the primary source and documents for all purposes. Copies of the official documents may be inspected and obtained in the Management Office. In the event of an error, misprint, omission, or conflict between the within versions and the official documents, the official printed and recorded documents shall govern and be controlling.
